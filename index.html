About the Series

A twintails-obsessed teenager named Souji Mitsuka finds himself embroiled in an intricate pastiche of tokusatsu & otaku culture while fending off the world’s least competent alien invasion.

If you can’t differentiate between maid & meido, you’re probably unprepared for the contents of this work. This is an aggressively Japanese piece of fiction.
