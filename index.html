// SERIES OVERVIEW

THE WORLD'S MOST
LUDICROUS
SUPERPOWER

Souji Mitsuka loves twintails more than anything. The alien invaders threatening Earth run on Attribute energy — the raw force of human passion and obsession. And passion for twintails, somehow, turns out to be the most powerful force in the universe.

Armed with the Tailgear and a burning conviction he'll never be able to explain to anyone, Souji transforms into Tailred — a crimson-armored warrior — and sets out to defend humanity. One hairstyle at a time.

A love letter to tokusatsu, otaku culture, and everything that is aggressively, unapologetically Japanese about anime. If you can't tell the difference between maid and meido, you're probably unprepared.
