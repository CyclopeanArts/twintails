Glossary

Glossary

Characters

Main Characters

Souji Mitsuka (観束 総二)

  • Protagonist and first-person narrator
  • Transforms into: Tailred
  • Speech patterns: Uses “ore” (masculine/casual). Direct, self-aware about his twintail obsession. Earnest despite awkwardness. More high-pitched when transformed.

Aika Tsube (筒隠 愛香)

  • Souji’s childhood friend
  • Transforms into: Tailblue
  • Speech patterns: Casual, direct, tomboyish. Uses “atashi” (feminine/casual).

Twoearle (トゥアール)

  • Alien warrior from another world
  • Provides the Tailgear transformation technology

Antagonists

Fox Guildy (フォックス・ギルディ)

  • Elemerian invader
  • Attributes: Ribbon + Doll
  • Appearance: Fox-like, speaks with voice-actor quality voice
  • First appearance: Chapter 3

Terminology

Tailgear (テイルギア)

  • Transformation armor that allows Souji to become Tailred
  • Powered by twintail attribute energy

Elemerian (エレメリアン)

  • Alien race that harvests human attribute energy
  • Each Elemerian specializes in specific attributes (fetishes)

Attribute Energy (属性力)

  • Spiritual energy generated by humans through their preferences and attractions
  • What Elemerians seek to harvest from Earth

Tailred (テイルレッド)

  • Souji’s hero name when transformed
  • Red twintailed warrior form

Tailblue (テイルブルー)

  • Aika’s hero name when transformed
  • Blue twintailed warrior form
  • First transformation: Chapter 3

Force Revon (フォース・リヴォン)

  • The ribbon parts of Tailgear worn on the twintails themselves

Spatial Leap Catapult (空間跳躍カタパルト)

  • Deployment system that covers the entire world
  • Allows rapid response to Elemerian attacks

Attributes

Twintail Attribute (ツインテール属性)

  • The primary attribute shared by both Souji and Aika
  • Souji’s pathological obsession; Aika wears her twintails “for someone else” (implied to be for Souji)
  • Powers both Tailred and Tailblue transformations
  • Aika is the second person in the world capable of equipping Tailgear due to her Twintail Attribute

Ribbon Attribute (リボン属性)

  • Fox Guildy’s primary attribute

Doll Attribute (人形属性)

  • Fox Guildy’s secondary attribute

This glossary will be updated as new characters and terminology appear in the translation.
